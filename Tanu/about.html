About Me

About me

Hi, I’m Tanushree Ojha! I’m a foodie at heart, an avid traveler, and a mehendi enthusiast with a passion for all things creative. This blog is my way of sharing the three things that bring me joy: exploring new destinations, trying delicious recipes, and expressing my love for intricate henna designs.

On this blog, you’ll find my travel stories from off-the-beaten-path locations, foodie adventures as I sample local cuisines, and a deep dive into the world of mehendi. Whether it's finding the best street food in a bustling market or creating stunning henna patterns for weddings and festivals, I’m here to inspire and share my experiences with you.

I believe that food, travel, and art all have the power to connect people and cultures, and I want to bring you along on this journey. If you’re looking for travel tips, mouth-watering recipes, or beautiful henna designs to try, you’ve come to the right place!
